About Resort

Camz & Ofel Island Resort is the first swimming pool resort established here at Talim island Binangonan Rizal with 4,526 sq, on target focus of safety and confort of the guest in their homeland.

The businesswas started to operate April 2018 owned by Mr. Avelino and Mrs. Ofelia Villadiego. They are working as fishpond operators and fish dealers. They have 3 daughter and finised their respective degrees.

Resort was fully registered in all government aspect with 5 employees.

Way back 2015 those land was bamboo field owned by other family and when they sold it first idea was poultry darm but to consider that this was near residential area they changed the plan and an engineer advised them to make it resort and give them proposal with design how wil it looks like. Even it is against all odds and many criticisms they pursue the business and established the resort.

Till now the resort is operating despite of the Pandemic, we followed the guidelines and Health Protocols and the Municipality of Binangonan gave us Safety Seal to operate.
